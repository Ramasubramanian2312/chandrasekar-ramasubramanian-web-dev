BoxShop - An online shopping store

BoxShop is a web-based retail shopping application that allows customers to browse for products, maintain a wishlist and a shopping cart, review and purchase products online. The application includes a Rest Client integrated with Walmart API for product search and PayPal API for making online payments on checkout.

The system comprises of five high level functionalities namely Search product, Manage Wishlist, Manage Shopping Cart, Manage Orders, Checkout and purchase products. Registered customers can review and provide feedback on products based on their experience.
